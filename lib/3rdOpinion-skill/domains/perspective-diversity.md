# Perspective Diversity Domain

Analyzes AI responses for missing viewpoints, cultural/context differences, and alternative interpretations.

## What to Look For

### Missing Perspectives
- Western/industrialized world bias
- Technical vs business perspective
- Short-term vs long-term view
- Individual vs collective framing
- Privilege/blind spots in viewpoint

### Cultural Factors
- Geographic/cultural assumptions
- Economic context differences
- Educational background assumptions
- Industry-specific perspectives

### Alternative Interpretations
- Other valid frameworks
- Opposing viewpoints
- Edge cases the response ignores
- Stakeholder perspectives not considered

## Trigger Indicators

Look for:
- Definitive recommendations without caveats
- One-size-fits-all solutions
- Assumptions about resources/constraints
- Lack of alternative options presented
- Singular "right way" framing

## Analysis Questions

1. Who would disagree with this perspective?
2. What cultural/context factors might change this?
3. What other stakeholders might have different views?
4. What geographic/economic contexts are assumed?
5. What alternatives exist that weren't mentioned?
6. What would someone with opposite view argue?

## Domain Weight Factors

Increase when:
- Response makes recommendations
- Contains advice or opinions
- Assumes particular context
- Presents single perspective

Decrease when:
- Response presents multiple viewpoints
- Includes caveats and alternatives
- Acknowledges context limitations
- Purely factual/informational content

## Common Perspective Gaps

- Developed vs developing world
- Large corporation vs startup
- Technical vs non-technical user
- Urban vs rural contexts
- Different religious/cultural backgrounds
- Different age/generation perspectives
- Management vs contributor level

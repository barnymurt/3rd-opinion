# Risk/Tradeoff Analysis Domain

Evaluates potential downsides, risks, and tradeoffs in AI responses.

## What to Look For

### Risk Categories

**Implementation Risks**
- Technical challenges
- Resource requirements
- Time/cost overruns
- Integration difficulties

**Unintended Consequences**
- Negative side effects
- Downstream impacts
- Second-order effects

**Opportunity Costs**
- What else could be done?
- Alternative uses of resources

**Failure Modes**
- When would this fail?
- What could go wrong?
- Edge cases not addressed

### Tradeoff Analysis
- What is being traded off?
- Who wins and who loses?
- Short-term vs long-term
- Local vs global optimization

## Trigger Indicators

Look for:
- Recommendations without caveats
- "Best" or "perfect" solutions
- Ignoring potential problems
- One-sided benefits
- No mention of alternatives

## Analysis Questions

1. What could go wrong?
2. What are the tradeoffs?
3. What is the opportunity cost?
4. When would this advice be wrong?
5. What are the second-order effects?
6. Who might be harmed by this?
7. What edge cases are missed?

## Domain Weight Factors

Increase when:
- Response makes recommendations
- Suggests courses of action
- Claims effectiveness
- Presents solutions

Decrease when:
- Response discusses tradeoffs
- Acknowledges risks
- Presents multiple options
- Informational content

## Common Risk Areas

- Technical debt
- Vendor lock-in
- Security vulnerabilities
- Scalability issues
- Maintenance burden
- User adoption challenges
- Regulatory/compliance risks

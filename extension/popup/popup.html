Preferences

What is Third Opinion?

Third Opinion helps you think more critically about AI responses by offering alternative perspectives and challenging confirmation bias.

How does it work?

When you're chatting with AI for 7+ minutes with few messages, we'll prompt you to get a second opinion. Or click the extension anytime to analyze the current conversation.

What are credits?

Free tier includes 20 opinions per month. Pro tier includes unlimited opinions and multiple AI perspectives.

How do I connect my AI APIs?

Go to Settings to add your API keys. This enables the extension to call different AI models for broader perspectives.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your conversations are processed locally and we don't store them. API keys are stored in your browser's secure storage.
